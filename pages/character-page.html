Albedo — An alchemist based in Mondstadt, in the service of the knights of Favonius. "Genius," "Kreideprinz," or "Captain of the Investigation Team"... Such titles and honors are of no consequence to him when there is so much more research to conduct. Luck or connections with important people were never hope in his heart, and his devotion was completely focused on the boundless knowledge left by the learned generations of yesteryear.

Mona — An astrologer who refers to himself as "Astrologist Mona Megistus", and possesses abilities that befit his nickname. His intelligence is equal to his arrogance. No matter how poor or difficult Mona's life is, she will never use her fortune-telling ability to make money... This life principle makes Mona always worried about her financial condition.

Lisa — A witch who is never satisfied with sleep. As a librarian at Knights of Favonius, Lisa always shows her intelligence in solving any problems that befall her. Even though she seems lazy and her love for naps seems over the top, Lisa can always make sure everything is under control.

Bennett — The young adventurers of the Adventurers' Guild in Mondstadt are always facing misfortune after misfortune. He is the only active member of his adventure squad, named "Benny's Adventure Team," after all the other members gave up one after another after an unfortunate incident. As a result, the adventurer team is now in danger of being disbanded.

Eula — A descendant of nobility who is nicknamed the Spindrift Knight and is always on the battlefield. As one born into the old aristocracy, carrying the bloodline of sinners, Eula has needed a unique approach to the world to navigate the towering walls of prejudice peacefully. Of course, this did not prevent her from severing ties with her clan. As the outstanding Spindrift Knight, she hunts down Mondstadt's enemies in the wild to exact her unique "vengeance."
